Our Boat Tours Details

Prepare for an extraordinary aquatic journey with The Royal View Boat Tours. Step aboard our remarkable vessel, View Cruiser, and let the enchanting waters of Torridon unveil their wonders before your eyes.
Our boat tours offer a diverse array of experiences, from tranquil scenic cruises to thrilling wildlife encounters. With experienced captains at the helm and a friendly crew to cater to your needs, safety and comfort are our top priorities. View Cruiser is equipped with modern amenities, ensuring a delightful voyage.
Explore the hidden gems of Torridon's waterways, witness mesmerizing landscapes, and encounter local wildlife in their natural habitat.
Join us for an adventure that promises to leave you with lasting memories of the beauty and serenity of the sea.




Discover the outdoors with our Hiking Club at The Royal View Hotel. Expert guides lead you through scenic trails, offering adventures for all skill levels. Explore Torridon's natural beauty and create lasting memories with fellow enthusiasts. Your gateway to adventure starts here.

Our Hiking Club Details

At The Royal View, we're passionate about outdoor exploration, and our Hiking Club is the perfect way to experience it firsthand. Our club is led by a team of experienced guides who are well-versed in the diverse trails and scenic wonders of Torridon.
We offer a variety of hikes catering to different skill levels, from leisurely walks to challenging ascents. Each hike is carefully planned, providing a safe and enjoyable experience for our members.
Whether you seek to connect with nature, take in stunning vistas, or simply enjoy the camaraderie of like-minded adventurers, our Hiking Club has something special to offer.
Join us on the trail and embark on unforgettable journeys into the heart of the wilderness.
